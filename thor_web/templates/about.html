About Thor

Thor Flashcards reinvents the idea of immersion at home. The most common way to learn a language is to read news articles and blogs in the language you're trying to learn. A tedium develops from the constant back-and-forth between on the one hand, translating words that are unfamiliar, and on the other hand, trying to remember these words. What happens is that because the process of translating the word is such a diversion from the source media, the context in which the word was used becomes forgotten, removing an integral part of the language learning experience.

Thor Flashcards aims to solve this problem by providing a suite of applications which enable fast translation of words through a plugin (for the Google Chrome browser), and the ability to directly add these translated words to a flashcard set. Current technologies allow for plugin translation (see WordReference Chrome Plugin), and other software allows for flashcard usage (see Anki, Memrise), but none combines the two functionalities and it is our belief that serving this niche vitally improves the fluidity in which one can immerse themselves in a language of their choice from their computer.
