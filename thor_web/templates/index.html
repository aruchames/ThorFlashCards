Get started with the product you've been waiting for!

Thor Flashcards has thought of everything you need to finally learn that language. Thor aims to replicate an immersion experience - a method proven to be the most effective way to learn a language.

When browsing media in the language of your choice, you will encounter words and idioms you aren't familiar with. With Thor's plugin integration all you have to do is right click the word, and add it to your deck. The translation to your native language will be done automatically. Then, later, you can review the decks you've created. It's as easy as that.
